### Verify Connectivity

First, confirm the target server is reachable on the specified port:

````
nc -vz mysterious-sea.picoctf.net 55226
````
<img width="1035" height="108" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6c6033c8-d305-4f5b-9ef7-49aacbf75cb0" />

This confirms the SMB service is listening. 


### Enumerate SMB share
Use ``smbclient`` with the ``-L`` flag to list available shares. The ``-N`` flag enables anonymous login (no password):
````
smbclient -L //mysterious-sea.picoctf.net -p 55226 -N
````
<img width="927" height="227" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3027ddb9-cf5c-42be-a971-eff22d7da5da" />

The ``shares`` directory is publicly accessible!

> Tip: Always enumerate shares with -L before guessing share names.



### Connect to the Public Share
Connect to the shares share using anonymous credentials:
````
smbclient //mysterious-sea.picoctf.net/shares -p 55226 -N
````
<img width="752" height="517" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/571048ad-a7c0-476a-8b0c-5da67ae779b2" />



### List Files in the Share
At the ``smb: \>`` prompt, list directory contents:
````
ls
````
<img width="778" height="151" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4a79ec50-bb74-4eff-8c24-763c6b9ec3ac" />

Now we see ``flag.txt``.


### Download the Flag
Use the ``get`` command to download the file:

````
get flag.txt
````
<img width="960" height="99" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3fd45e57-e3c2-4cc1-8822-27f4a7d18ff3" />

After open new Terminal by press `Ctrl + Shift + T`:
````
ls
cat flag.txt
````
<img width="351" height="211" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/364548f8-9bb7-4489-b986-b46522c4e8ac" />

Now we got the correct flag.
